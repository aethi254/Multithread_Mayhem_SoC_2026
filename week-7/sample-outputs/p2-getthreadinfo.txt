$ threadinfo_test
=== Thread Info Test ===

Before fork:
  pid=3  tgid=3  is_thread=0

Forking...

[parent] pid=3  tgid=3  is_thread=0
[child]  pid=4  tgid=4  is_thread=0

Check: for an ordinary process, tgid should equal pid (it is its own,
one-member thread group). Parent: tgid==pid -> true. Child: tgid==pid
-> true.

NOTE: pid values above (3 and 4) depend on how many processes booted
before this test ran (init and the shell already occupy earlier pids).
What matters is that parent and child each get their own tgid, equal
to their own pid, since no thread-sharing exists yet this week.
